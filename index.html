NOTES

Combat Range : It is the maximum range of an aircraft when carrying ordinance.

Combat Radius : It is a related measure based on the maximum distance an aircraft can travel from its base of operations , accomplish some objective and return to its original airfield with minimal reserves.

Service Ceiling : It is the maximum altitude after which an aircraft cannot climb faster than a given rate.

Ferry Range : It is the maximum distance an aircraft can travel with maximum fuel and no ordinance.
